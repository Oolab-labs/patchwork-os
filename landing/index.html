AI that works while
you're away.

Patchwork OS watches for things that matter, acts, and asks before anything risky goes out. Runs on your machine. Doesn't lock you in.

Get started → View recipes 
$ npm install -g patchwork-os
$ patchwork init
How it works
Three steps to autopilot.
1

Install

One command. Detects your tools, sets up 5 starter recipes, opens the dashboard.

2

Connect

Link Gmail, Slack, Linear, GitHub and 10+ more. OAuth in one click.

3

Approve

Patchwork acts. Anything risky lands in your inbox first. You tap once.

Features
Everything you need. Nothing you don't.
📄

Recipes

Plain YAML files describe what to watch and what to do. Share them like dotfiles. No code required.

🛡

Oversight

Every write, send, or commit lands in your approval queue before it goes anywhere.

⚡

Multi-model

Claude, GPT, Gemini, Grok, or local Ollama. Your keys, your models. Nothing phones home.

🏪

Marketplace

Browse and install community recipes in one command.
patchwork recipe install github:patchworkos/recipes/...
